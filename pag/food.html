Jordanian Food

"Must try" dishes and where to find them !

Navigate to resturants advised by locals by clicking on the "Visit" button:

Hummus

Dip, spread, or savory dish made from cooked, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic.

Visit Abu Jbara
Falafel

Deep-fried balls made from ground chickpeas, fava beans, or both.

Visit Alquds
Kibbeh

spiced ground meat and grain.

Visit Ward
Knafeh

Traditional Middle Eastern dessert made with fine semolina dough, soaked in sweet, sugar-based syrup, and layered with cheese

Visit Habiba Sweets
Warak enab

Grape leaves stuffed with rice and meat or rice and veggies.

Visit Warak enab
Galayet bandoora

Simple dish of tomatoes, onions, hot peppers, olive oil, and salt.

Visit Khashouka
Manaqeesh

Special dough topped with thyme, cheese, or ground meat.

Qatayef

sweet dumpling filled with cream or nuts..
